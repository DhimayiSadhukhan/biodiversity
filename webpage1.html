BIODIVERSITY

Earth provides enough to satisfy every man's needs, but not every man's greed-MAHATMA GANDHI

The diverse variety of plants,animals along with microorganism living in a particular region is called biodiversity.

Importance of Biodiversity

The variety of life on Earth, its biological diversity is commonly referred to as biodiversity.Biodiversity boosts ecosystem productivity where each species, no matter how small, all have an important role to play.Biodiversity holds ecological and economic significance. It provides us with nourishment, housing, fuel, clothing and several other resources.

Causes of Loss of Biodiversity

  1. Deforestation
  2. Illegal hunting
  3. Pollution
  4. Natural Disasters
  5. Exotic species
